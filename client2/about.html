About McDonald's

Our History

Founded in 1940 by brothers Richard and Maurice McDonald, McDonald's revolutionized fast food. Ray Kroc joined in 1954, expanding globally. Now a global icon, McDonald's serves millions daily.

Our Mission

At McDonald's, we are committed to providing quality food and a great dining experience. We strive to make our customers happy with every meal.
